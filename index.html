What it is

Dinner Deck is a physical tool that removes decision fatigue. It tells you what you’re making so you can move on with your night.

What it isn’t

It’s not a cookbook, a diet, or an app. It doesn’t lecture, optimize, or nag.

Hack your deck

Don’t eat certain foods? Have allergies or religious constraints? Remove the cards that don’t apply before you put the deck on the counter.
